About the weather city tool

The Weather City Tool is an easy-to-use application designed to provide real-time weather information for any city around the world. With just a city name, users can instantly access up-to-date weather data, including:

  • Current Temperature: See the temperature in your preferred units (Celsius or Fahrenheit).
  • Weather Conditions: View the general weather conditions (e.g., sunny, rainy, cloudy).
  • Humidity and Wind Speed: Understand the humidity levels and wind speed in the selected city.
